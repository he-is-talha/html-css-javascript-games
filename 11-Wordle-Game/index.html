How to play

Guess the hidden word in a limited number of tries.

Each guess must be a valid word. Type with the keyboard or tap the on-screen keys, then press Enter.

After each guess, the tiles show:

  • W Green — letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • E Yellow — letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • A Gray — letter is not in the word.

Choose a level: Easy (4 letters), Medium (5), Hard (6), or Expert (7). Turn on Show hints for a small clue about the word.
